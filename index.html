About me

This wonderful journey in the 3D design world started several years ago from when I was a high school student, in my technical design subject class, where I was studying AutoCad AutoDesk 2008 software, mainly doing 3D design of various components using blueprint of each component. In 2019 after a long break from the world of 3D design, I decided to challenge myself once again, discovering Blender 3D software and started to learn mainly hard surface modeling, like voxel art and environmental components. Once a subject in school, became a true passion for design! But my interest in design didn't stop there, and I started studying Front-end development, web design using mainly HTML, CSS, JS.

goals

As an idealist I always try to break through my limits, always finding solutions to problems in a creative way! That being said my goal is to become a full-fledged 3D designer, web designer, front-end developer, continuing to learn, grow and strive to become the best version of myself! And hopefully one day becoming able to help others achieve their dreams of becoming future designers!
