About me

As an idealist I always try to break through my limits, striving for a better version of myself. This wonderful journey in the 3D design world started several years ago from when I was a high school student, in my technical design subject class, where I was studying AutoCad AutoDesk 2000 software, mainly doing 3D design of the various components using the blueprint of each component.
In 2019 after a long break from the world of 3D design, I decided to challenge myself once again, discovering Blender 3D software and started to learn mainly hard surface modeling, like voxel art and environmental components. Once a subject in school became a true passion for design!
But my interest in design didn't stop there, and I started studying Front-end development, web design using mainly HTML, CSS, JS.

goals

My goal is to become a full-fledged 3D designer, web designer, front-end developer, continuing to learn, grow and strive to become able to help others achieve their dreams of becoming a designer!
